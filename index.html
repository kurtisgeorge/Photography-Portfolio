Still Life, Calgary.

My Style

I’m drawn to scenes that feel fleeting, imperfect, or in-between — the quiet gestures, soft glances, and ordinary patterns that often go unnoticed. My approach is observational and instinctive. Whether it's someone paused mid-step, a blur of motion, or the weight of a shadow, I aim to preserve what most people would walk past. Street photography, to me, is a form of emotional documentation — it’s not about perfection, but about presence.
